Benefits of Interactive and Laser Cat Toys

Interactive cat toys encourage your feline friend to follow their natural instincts to chase, pounce and stalk! When your cat is outdoors, they have plenty of freedom exploring their surrounding flora and fauna, however, when they're indoors, access to nature is limited which is why an alternative source of stimulation is vital for your cat's health & wellbeing. Interactive cat toys replicate the movement and size of natural prey like birds and mice, encouraging your cat to 'hunt'.
