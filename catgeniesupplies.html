CatGenie Supplies & Accessories

The CatGenie is an easy to use automatic cat litter box that requires very little maintenance to run effectively! Your CatGenie unit requires 2 supplies to begin a cleaning cycle; SaniSolution and Washable Granules. SaniSolution is an eco-friendly formula designed to sanitise the CatGenie bowl and Granules keeping your cat's toileting area clean and fresh. One SaniSolution cartridge lasts up to 4 months with one cat. Available in Fresh Scent or Scent Free.

The Washable Granules never need changing or scooping! One box of Washable Granules lasts up to 4 months. How? The Granules are made from a non-absorbent combination of natural and synthetic materials. The Granules are completely safe for pets and people, and unlike other forms of cat litter that can take quite a huge toll on the environment, CatGenie Granules are environmentally friendly and will naturally degrade in septic systems in just 9 months.
